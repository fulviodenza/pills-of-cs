`Pills of computer science` is a bot to get "pills" of computer science, such as
definitions, curiosity, and best practices in small pills, news about
computer science world (to be implemented) and other stuff TBD.

`/start`: To start using the bot run;
`/pills`: To get a random pill;
`/choose_tags arg1 arg2 ...`: To subscribe to one or more categories from `/get_tags`;
`/get_tags`: Get all available tags you can subscribe to;
`/get_subscribed_tags`: Get all tags you're subscribed to.
